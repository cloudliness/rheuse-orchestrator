### Where do you ship?
We currently ship within the United States. We're working on expanding internationally — sign up for our newsletter and we'll let you know when that changes.

### How long does shipping take?
Most orders ship within 1–2 business days. Standard delivery typically takes 3–5 business days once shipped. You'll receive a tracking link by email as soon as your order is on its way.

### Is shipping free?
Orders over $50 ship free within the US. For orders under $50, a flat shipping rate is applied at checkout.

### What's your return policy?
If something isn't right, you have 30 days from delivery to return or exchange it. Products should be unused and in their original packaging. We'll cover return shipping for defective items. For all other returns, a prepaid return label is available at a small fee. Reach out to us and we'll make it easy.

### Are your products really sustainable?
We don't use the word "sustainable" without backing it up. Every product page lists the specific material, its certification (like FSC, GOTS, or BPA-free ratings), and what it replaces. For example, our silicone food bags are rated for 3,000+ uses each, and our organic cotton totes are made from GOTS-certified cotton with AZO-free dyes. If a product includes a material that isn't fully compostable — like nylon-6 bristles on our bamboo toothbrushes — we say so.

### How do I care for my Rheuse products?
Care varies by material:
- **Stainless steel** (bottles, mugs, straws, razors) — dishwasher safe. Hand wash with warm soapy water for best longevity.
- **Borosilicate glass** (bottles, cups, straws) — dishwasher safe. Thermal shock resistant, but avoid sudden extreme temperature changes.
- **Bamboo** (utensils, toothbrushes) — hand wash and air dry. Do not soak or put in the dishwasher.
- **Organic cotton** (totes, pouches, produce bags) — machine wash cold, tumble dry low or air dry.
- **Silicone** (food bags, stretch lids, sleeves) — dishwasher safe. Can also be boiled for deep cleaning.
- **Beeswax wraps** — wash with cool water and mild soap. Avoid hot water, microwaves, and raw meat.

Specific care instructions are included with every product.

### How is my order packaged?
We use minimal, recyclable packaging — kraft paper, recycled cardboard, and paper tape. No plastic fillers, no styrofoam, no excess. Your products arrive protected, not buried in waste.
